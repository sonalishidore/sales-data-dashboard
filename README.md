# 📊 Retail Business Analytics Dashboard

## 🔍 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing retail business data to generate meaningful insights using interactive dashboards. It helps in understanding sales trends, customer behavior, and operational efficiency.

---

## 🎯 Business Problems Solved

### 1️⃣ Top Categories by Total Price
- **Visualization:** Stacked Bar Chart  
- Identifies top-performing product categories based on total sales value.

---

### 2️⃣ Delayed Order Analysis
- **Visualization:** Clustered Bar Chart  
- Compares delayed vs on-time orders to identify delivery issues.

---

### 3️⃣ Monthly Comparison of Orders
- **Visualization:** Card, Slicer, Stacked Column Chart  
- Tracks monthly performance of delayed and on-time deliveries.

---

### 4️⃣ Payment Method Analysis
- **Visualization:** Pie Chart  
- Shows most frequently used payment methods by customers.

---

### 5️⃣ Product Rating Analysis
- **Visualization:** Stacked Column Chart  
- Identifies top-rated and lowest-rated products.

---

### 6️⃣ State-wise Sales Analysis
- **Visualization:** Map  
- Displays regional sales performance across states.

---

### 7️⃣ Seasonal Sales Patterns
- **Visualization:** Line Chart  
- Analyzes quarterly sales trends and seasonality.

---

### 8️⃣ Revenue Analysis
- **Visualization:** Line & Bar Chart  
- Tracks revenue trends over time and across product categories.

---
